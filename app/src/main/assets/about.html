About Page

When a company grows in size and the number of employees increase, keeping track of all employees might become a problem.

This problem is something that the HR-Department here at Intended-nihility have been struggling with for a while now, which have lead to the development of this application and tool.

The application is designed to increase the efficiency and decrease the required workload of the HR-Departments everyday tasks, by providing a clean and easy way to browse the basic information about the employees, like contact information and position etc.
